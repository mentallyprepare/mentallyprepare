Terms of Service

Last updated: March 21, 2026

The short version: This is an anonymous emotional journaling service. Use it respectfully, do not abuse anonymity, and do not treat it as emergency or clinical care.

1. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old to use Mentally Prepare, or use it only with parent or guardian consent where legally permitted. By creating an account, you confirm that you meet this requirement.

2. Acceptable Use

You agree to use the service in a way that protects the safety and anonymity of other users.

  • No harassment, intimidation, stalking, bullying, or abuse
  • No sharing of phone numbers, email addresses, social handles, or other contact details
  • No impersonation of another person, institution, or identity

3. Anonymous Content

Your journal entries are shared only with your matched partner. Content is not public, not searchable, and not designed for social distribution. You are responsible for what you write, even when you remain unnamed.

4. Safety

We may surface crisis-support information when content indicates self-harm or acute distress. Users may report content they believe is unsafe. We reserve the right to restrict access or remove accounts when safety requires it.

5. Prohibited Content

  • Hate speech or content targeting protected groups
  • Sexually explicit, exploitative, or coercive content
  • Threats of violence, extortion, or encouragement of harm

6. Account Termination

We may suspend or remove accounts that violate these terms, abuse platform features, or create risk for other users. We may also disable access where required for legal or operational reasons.

7. Limitation of Liability

Mentally Prepare is a reflective journaling product — not therapy, medical advice, psychiatric treatment, emergency response, or crisis intervention. To the maximum extent permitted by law, you use the service at your own risk.

8. Contact

Questions: terms@mentallyprepare.in

Terms of Service

Effective March 2026

1. What this service is

Mentally Prepare is a 21-day anonymous journal experience. You write daily, are matched with a student from another college, and can choose to reveal your identity at the end.

2. Age requirement

All users must be 18 years or older to participate.

3. Acceptable use

  • No harassment, bullying, or abusive behavior.
  • No explicit or sexual content.
  • No sharing of personal contact details to bypass anonymity.

4. Account termination

We reserve the right to ban or terminate accounts that violate these terms.

5. No guarantee of being matched

We do not guarantee that you will be matched with a partner within a specific timeframe.

6. Not a mental health service

This is not a mental health service or crisis line. If you need urgent help, please contact a professional or a crisis helpline.

7. Contact

For questions or concerns, email privacy@mentallyprepare.in.
